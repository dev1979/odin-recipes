Quick and Easy Lemon Chicken

This Quick and Easy Lemon Chicken Recipe requires only a handful of pantry ingredients, but don't let the simplicity fool you — it's outrageously delicious!

Why you would love this recipe!

  • Delicious: Easy to make using pantry staple ingredients.
  • Versatile and Pleasing: Ideal for both casual dinners and special occasions, loved by adults and kids alike.
  • Quick to Prepare: A convenient choice for busy weeknights.
  • Pairs Well with Many Sides: Complements a variety of side dishes for a complete, satisfying meal.

Ingredients

  • 4 boneless skinless chicken breasts
  • 1/2 cup lemon juice about 2 lemons
  • 2 tablespoons extra virgin olive oil
  • 2 teaspoons lemon pepper
  • 1 teaspoon dried basil
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • ¼ teaspoon salt

Steps

  1. Place chicken breasts in a gallon-size resealable plastic bag. Add lemon juice, olive oil, lemon pepper, basil, oregano, and salt to the bag. Shake the bag gently to ensure the chicken is evenly coated with the marinade.
  2. Marinate in the refrigerator for 30 minutes, or up to 8 hours for deeper flavor.
  3. Heat a heavy skillet over medium-high heat. Remove chicken from the marinade and place in the hot skillet. Discard leftover marinade.
  4. Cook chicken for 6 to 7 minutes on each side, flipping once halfway through.
  5. Continue cooking until the chicken reaches an internal temperature of 165°F.
  6. Serve hot. Optionally, garnish with freshly chopped parsley and lemon slices.
